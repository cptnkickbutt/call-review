Welcome to Call Review

This page helps you find, review, and listen to recorded calls. Use the search bar and filters to narrow the list, then click a file name or ID to open the full call page.

How to use this page

  • Search: Find calls by filename, transcript text, phone number, or keywords.
  • System: Limit results to CX or VIPVoice calls.
  • Tag: Show only calls matching a specific tag.
  • Date filters: Narrow results to a date range.
  • Click a call: Open the detail page to review transcript, audio, notes, and tags.
  • Top Tags: Quickly jump into common call topics.

Status guide

  • queued: waiting to be processed
  • processing: currently being transcribed or prepared
  • complete: ready for review
  • failed: something went wrong and may need attention

Transcripts, summaries, and tags are auto-generated and may not always be accurate. Please verify important details before relying on them.
